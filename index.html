WILD AT HEART

Why we exist

We believe food is more than just something you eat—it’s a connection to life, to nature, and to the people who grow it. In a world that feels increasingly disconnected, we exist to bring food closer to its roots. Our purpose is simple: to nourish not just bodies, but relationships with the earth and each other. We do this by growing fresh, local produce that inspires people to live healthier, more sustainable lives.

We are Wild at Heart.

Why mushrooms

Mushrooms are more than just an ingredient—they are nature’s gift to our health. Packed with nutrients, vitamins, and antioxidants, mushrooms are true superfoods that boost immunity and promote overall well-being.

In today’s world, where health is our greatest priority, specialty mushrooms stand out for their unique ability to nourish our bodies while delighting our taste buds. We focus on growing exotic varieties that are not only rich in benefits but also versatile and delicious—making them an ideal choice for your kitchen. Each mushroom we cultivate reflects our commitment to health, sustainability, and the joy of good food.

How we do it

We’ve redefined the way mushrooms and microgreens are grown. With our cutting-edge Micro-Farm Architecture powered by kaaniOS, we cultivate fresh, exotic produce in compact urban farms, bringing nature’s best right into your neighborhood.

Our farms are designed to operate sustainably, following a net-zero approach that minimizes environmental impact. Every detail—from resource efficiency to optimized growing conditions—is fine-tuned to ensure purity, freshness, and flavor in every harvest.

By blending organic farming principles with modern technology, we’re creating a future where fresh, healthy food is always within reach, benefiting you and the planet alike.

Our Vision

To create a world where food reconnects us to the earth and to each other. At Akaththi Farms, we dream of communities where fresh, local produce is grown nearby and enjoyed with purpose. We envision a future where every meal celebrates the beauty of nature and the value of sustainability.

Our Mission

To transform the way food is grown and enjoyed by building small, sustainable urban farms that bring fresh, nutritious produce directly to our community. We focus on growing exotic mushrooms and microgreens using responsible, innovative practices that protect the planet while nourishing healthier lifestyles.

What we offer

We bring the farm to your table with a selection of fresh, exotic mushrooms and vibrant microgreens, grown with care and delivered just hours after harvest. Every bite carries the unmistakable taste of freshness, backed by our commitment to sustainable, hyper-local farming.

Our mushrooms are cultivated to perfection, offering rich, earthy flavors and exceptional nutritional benefits. Alongside, our microgreens add a burst of flavor and vitality to any dish, making it easy to elevate your meals with health and taste.

Everything we grow reflects our promise of quality, innovation, and sustainability—ensuring you enjoy produce that’s as good for the planet as it is for you.
